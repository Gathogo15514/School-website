Welcome to Nanyuki Elimu Academy

Nanyuki Elimu Academy, located in the serene environment of Laikipia County, is dedicated to providing a holistic education that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century. Founded in 2010, we have grown to become a leading primary school in the region, known for our commitment to academic excellence and character development.

Our Mission: To provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child can reach their full potential.

Our Vision: To be a center of excellence in primary education, shaping future leaders.

School Motto: Education is the Key.

"At Nanyuki Elimu Academy, we believe in fostering a love for learning and nurturing well-rounded individuals. We are committed to providing a safe and supportive environment where every child can thrive."
